Our Philosophy

The Royal Lineage

Where French Rococo Meets Royal Rajputana

A beautiful, dark romantic fusion. Combining the soft pastel curves of Rococo feminine details with the grand gilded architecture of old Jaipur courtrooms.

Anindé & Co. was established with a singular vision: to treat home fragrances as a sacred artistic ritual. Inspired by the rich sensory archive of the Maharani palaces of Rajasthan and the vintage pastel florals of European Rococo salons, we set out to create candles that act as visual and aromatic poetry.

Each blend is crafted slowly. We utilize deep wine tones, antique gold details, and faded gradients of maroon to paint a canvas of romance, history, and noble elegance.

Alchemical Devotion

The Crafting Rituals

Pure Wax Alchemy

We hand-pour pure soy and coconut wax blends. Completely free of paraffin, toxins, or additives, guaranteeing an incredibly slow, clean, and soot-free burn.

Sacred Incense Oils

Sourced from sacred flower-cycling temples and absolute rose distillers in Kannauj. Our signature scents carry deep, complex bases of aged sandalwood and temple myrrh.

Gilded Vessels

Our vessels are individually cast in brass and hand-etched by metalsmiths. Designed to be reused for generations as jewelry holders or vintage vanity trinkets.

Lunar Curing

Candles undergo a mandatory 21-day curing process under specific celestial phases to ensure oil molecules bind perfectly, ensuring deep scent throw.

Generational Support

By selecting Anindé & Co., you are supporting over 120 generational metalsmiths, clay-potters, and flower collectors across Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh.

We dedicate 10% of every sale to support the education of metal artisans' children, ensuring that the legacy of hand-hammered Indian metalwork is carried gracefully into the modern era.
