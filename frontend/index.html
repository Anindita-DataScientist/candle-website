The Essence of Warmth

Handcrafted Indian Heritage

Every candle is a testament to the passage of time—hand-rolled, hand-polished, and poured under specific lunar alignments.

Our brand is born at the intersection of Rococo's feminine softness and royal Indian heritage. Taking cues from the high-arched court corridors of palaces and the fragrant temple lanes, we source pure soy wax, natural botanicals, and hand-carved brass vessels from generational metalsmiths in Moradabad.

For us, a candle is not merely a home lighting item. It is a slow incense ritual, a romantic evening companion, and a gift of warm blessing. Grace your chambers with the fragrance of kings and queens.
